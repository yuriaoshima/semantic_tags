Yuri Aoshima

Basic Information

Yuri Aoshima was born on the blazing hot night of August 9th of 1993. She is of Chinese ethnicity even though her name is Japanese. She graduated from the University of Washington with a degree in electrical engineering focusing on embedded systems programing. She currenlty works full time at the Boeing Company. On her free time she loves to read, hike, sleep, and spend time with her boyfriend.

Why is Yuri's Name Japanese?

Yuri's name is Japanese because before she was born her parents lived in Japan and became Japanese citizens. In Japan, you must change your name to a Japanese name to become a citizen.

Yuri's Childhood

Yuri was born in Dalian, China which is North of Beijing. For the first four years of her life, she moved back and forth between Tokyo and Dalian since her granparents lived in Dalian and her dad lived in Tokyo. When Yuri was 4 she moved to Dalian and lived there for two years. She then moved to San Francisco where she resided for a year before moving to Las Vegas, NV. She lived in Las Vegas until the end of her senior year in high school in which she then moved to Seattle to attend the University of Washington.

College Days

Yuri entered the University of Washington with the intent of majoring in a life science of some sort to get her into medical school. Medical school was not her idea but something her parents strongly wanted her to do. After spending a year taking pre-med classes and shadowing doctors and surgeons, Yuri realized she did not want to become a doctor and switched to engineering. She found her passion in embedded systems her junior year of college. Yuri graduated in 2016 with a BS in EE cum laude.

Yuri's Life After Graduation

Yuri is currently a full time engineer at the Boeing Company and participates in the company's Engineering Career Foundation Program where she rotates between different groups. She has a passion for learning, working with programming languages and embedded systems. On her free time, Yuri likes to try new recipes, workout, sleep, watch Netflix, and spend time with her boyfriend DTan.
